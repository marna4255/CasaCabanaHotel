About

This property is 1 minute walk from the beach. Set in a quiet location in the centre of Faliraki and just a 3-minute walk from the beach, Casa Cabana Hotel & Suites provides rooms with free WiFi. Among the various facilities of this property are a garden, a bar and on-site dining. The hotel offers garden views and an outdoor pool.

At the hotel, all rooms are fitted with a balcony. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hair dryer. At Casa Cabana Hotel & Suites every room has a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV.

A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed at the property.

Speaking German and Greek at the reception, staff are always on hand to help.

Casa Cabana Hotel & Suites is 2 km of Anthony Quinn Bay and 3.2 km of Rhodes Waterpark. The nearest airport is Rhodes International Airport, 12 km from the accommodation.
